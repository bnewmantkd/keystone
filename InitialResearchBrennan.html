Initial Research

Exclusivity in STEM


Resources at and out of school are not enough to prepare many people for a world saturated with science and technology, especially with insufficient interest and accessibility.

Many people in occupations with sensitive documents sent through e-mail do not have a clear idea how it works.

Duolingo and Brilliant are great examples of making learning interesting through a practice called 'gamification'.

Scientific Illiteracy


Even those that aren't interested in a STEM career still need access to fun, helpful resources in science and technology to minimise misconceptions and ussues arising from ignorance.
